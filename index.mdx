## Our Story
EM Skills Enterprise CIC was established in 2016 providing training & tuition to children and young adults. In 2020 we became EM Tuition expanded our services to provide online tuition and education programmes to a wider range of people. We are dedicated to delivering comprehensive & innovative tuition & training programmes to inspire a powerful impact on the community. 


## Our Mission
EM Tuition was founded with a clear and ambitious mission: **to close the educational inequality gap by providing inclusive services for all Children and Young People (CYP).** We provide high-quality tuition for vulnerable learners and we firmly believe that every child, regardless of their circumstances or challenges, deserves a safe and engaging learning environment where they can thrive both academically and personally.

Our provision is designed to build confidence, develop relationships, and help the CYPs we work with to progress in life and education.

Your role as a tutor is at the heart of this mission, and this guide is designed to be your ultimate resource for success.

## Our Passion & Ethos

At EM Tuition, our passion is delivering exceptional educational support for learners who face barriers to mainstream schooling. We specialise in creating tailored programmes for students with anxiety, ADHD, autism, and other social, emotional, and mental health (SEMH) needs.

Our core ethos is built on three pillars:
1.  **Building Confidence:** We foster self-esteem and resilience in every learner.
2.  **Fostering Positive Relationships:** We create safe, trusting connections that form the foundation of learning.
3.  **Empowering Futures:** We equip students with the academic and personal skills to broaden their future choices.
