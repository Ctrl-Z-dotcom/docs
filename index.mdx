## Our Mission & Ethos

At EM Tuition, our passion is delivering exceptional educational support for learners who face barriers to mainstream schooling. We specialise in creating tailored programmes for students with anxiety, ADHD, autism, and other social, emotional, and mental health (SEMH) needs.

Our core ethos is built on three pillars:
1.  **Building Confidence:** We foster self-esteem and resilience in every learner.
2.  **Fostering Positive Relationships:** We create safe, trusting connections that form the foundation of learning.
3.  **Empowering Futures:** We equip students with the academic and personal skills to broaden their future choices.
