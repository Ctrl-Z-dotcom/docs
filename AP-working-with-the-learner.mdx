### Alternative provision: working with the learner
#### Initial contact and setup
<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Meet & greet visit" icon="handshake">
    Use the learner information form to contact families and arrange your initial visit. During this meeting:<br/>
    
      - Agree contact times with parent/guardian<br/>
      - Finalise session timetable and location<br/>
      - Return all details to your Account Manager before starting tuition<br/>
    
  </Card>
  <Card title="Relationship building" icon="users">
    Build rapport during your learner introduction by:
    <ul>
      <li>Discussing behaviour expectations (mutual)</li>
      <li>Learning about the learner's interests</li>
      <li>Identifying best learning methods</li>
      <li>Conducting risk assessment</li>
      <li>Reviewing attendance and safeguarding policies</li>
      <li>Sharing the learner handbook</li>
    </ul>
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
#### Safety and risk assessment
<Warning>
  **Important:** You must not transport a learner in your vehicle unless explicitly authorised by EM Tuition.
</Warning>
<Accordion title="Tuition outside the home">
  <ul>
    <li>Meet in public places whenever possible (libraries, coffee shops, suitable venues)</li>
    <li>Complete a Risk Assessment beforehand, considering learner needs and location-specific risks</li>
    <li>Ensure the venue is appropriate for educational activities</li>
  </ul>
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Home-based tuition">
  <ul>
    <li><strong>Mandatory:</strong> Complete risk assessment with parent/carer</li>
    <li><strong>Mandatory:</strong> Parent or guardian must always be present in the home</li>
    <li>If you feel nervous or compromised, contact the office immediately</li>
    <li>Report any safeguarding concerns to the DSL and Account Manager without delay</li>
  </ul>
</Accordion>
