## Delivery modes at a glance

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Face-to-face (in-school)" icon="building-2">
    <ul>
      <li>Bring your <strong>DBS</strong> and a valid <strong>photo ID</strong> on your first day.</li>
      <li>Provide <strong>weekly timesheets</strong>, signed by a member of school staff.</li>
    </ul>
  </Card>
  <Card title="Online" icon="monitor">
    <ul>
      <li>You'll receive email <strong>reminders</strong> 4 hours and 15 minutes before each session.</li>
