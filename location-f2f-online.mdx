<Info>
  Your role may involve **online** or **face-to-face** tuition. Use this page to confirm what’s expected in each case.
</Info>

## Delivery modes at a glance

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Face-to-face (in-school)" icon="building-2">

- Bring your **DBS** and a valid **photo ID** on your first day.
- Provide **weekly timesheets**, signed by a member of school staff.

  </Card>

  <Card title="Online" icon="monitor">

- You’ll receive email **reminders** 4 hours and 15 minutes before each session.
- Click the **Join** link in the email to open our online platform, **Lesson Space**.
- Before your first online session, review:
  - [Lesson Space tutorial series](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=MbiI7ka2roE&list=PL5N9Z0iwMlavipYZWR86ic23rySq6MBSU&ab_channel=Lessonspace)
  - [Lesson Space demo](https://www.thelessonspace.com/demo)

  </Card>
</CardGroup>
