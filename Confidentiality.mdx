## Confidential discussions

<div style={{
  padding: 20,
  borderRadius: 14,
  background: 'linear-gradient(180deg, rgba(10,132,255,0.08), rgba(10,132,255,0.03))',

  <ol style={{ margin: 0, paddingLeft: 18 }}>
Discussions between the learner and EM tuition staff are confidential unless there is a safeguarding issue. Staff have a duty to report any concerns about a learner to the Designated Safeguarding Lead (DSL) for Child Protection (Louise Georgiou). When communicating with anyone, regarding the learner, ensure that use only use initials or the first name. The learner should never be referred to, in any communication, by their full name.
