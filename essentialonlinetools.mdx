## 🧠 Tutor Cruncher

**TC **is the central hub for all your teaching roles. All your jobs, whether online or face-to-face, can be found here. It's where you will mark sessions as complete or cancelled, track learner attendance and write session reports. 

Getting to grips with TC will make your tutoring role smoother and more efficient. Check our 5-minute TC Tutorial.

- **Job Application. **Find new roles and apply for them.
- **Job Management**: Access all your assignments, whether online or face-to-face.
- **Attendance & Reports**: Mark sessions as _Complete_ or Cancelled and submit session reports.
- **Payroll Impact**: Timely updates directly affect your pay. All your sessions must be on TC to ensure you are paid for them.

> 📺 **Recommended**: Watch our **5-minute Tutor Cruncher tutorial** to get started quickly.

## 💻 Lesson Space

**Lesson Space** is our online teaching platform. **If you plan to conduct lessons online**, you're required to learn and use this tool.

- Host lessons with interactive **video conferencing**, **whiteboard tools**, and **document sharing**.
- Learn to assist learners with **basic technical troubleshooting** (e.g. browser/permissions errors).

> 🎥 Access the **Lesson Space Tutorial Series** and try the **Lesson Space Demo** to practice essential skills before teaching.

---

## ⚠️ Which Platform Applies to You?

- If you **don’t teach online**, prioritize **Tutor Cruncher only**.
- If applying for an **online tutoring role**, you **must** learn both Tutor Cruncher **and** Lesson Space.
