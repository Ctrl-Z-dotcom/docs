# Meet the EM Tuition Team

Welcome\! You're now part of a UK-wide network of professionals dedicated to changing the lives of young learners. The team below is your core support system, working behind the scenes to ensure you have everything you need to succeed. From leadership to operations and contract management, we are all here with a shared passion for closing the educational inequality gap.
